Lee Ji-eun

IU (Lee Ji-eun), a South Korean singer, songwriter, and actress. She debuted in 2008 and is best known for her songs like “Good Day” and “Palette.” She has also had the pleasure of starring in K-dramas such as "Moon Lovers" and "My Ahjussi." She is passionate about creating music and bringing characters to life on screen, and I’m grateful for the love and support.
